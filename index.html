Reporting rules

Useful without becoming invasive

  • No officer faces, names, badge numbers, vehicle plates, or private chat screenshots.
  • Use area-level locations and time windows instead of exact step-by-step movement.
  • Do not encourage evasion, confrontation, harassment, or interference with enforcement duties.
  • Assume every report is approximate until multiple independent sightings confirm it.
